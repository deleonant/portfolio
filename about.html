Defining Who We Are Through Music.

Music can support and enrich the development of a positive self-identity as well as provide confidence, motivation and a sense of belonging. Music can enhance creative, social and emotional skills. Music can be both a sense of self-preservation and fundamental wellbeing , providing a source of support when youth feel stressed, troubled or lonely .
